Project Name: Project Synergia
Industry: Human Resources / Enterprise SaaS
The Client Challenge: A global enterprise software company's internal recruiting team was overwhelmed by the volume of applicants. Recruiter burnout was high because they manually reviewed thousands of resumes against complex technical job descriptions. Their legacy Applicant Tracking System (ATS) only offered basic keyword matching, which frequently discarded highly qualified candidates who used different terminology.
Our Technical Solution: We built a modern, AI-driven recruitment copilot that integrated seamlessly with their existing ATS via API microservices. The frontend interface for the recruiters was built using Next.js, providing a blazing-fast, server-side rendered dashboard to manage applicant pipelines.
The core intelligence engine was developed as a Python/FastAPI microservice. When a new resume was uploaded, we used PyPDF2 for text extraction and OpenAI's embedding models to convert the applicant's experience, skills, and project descriptions into dense vectors. We stored these vectors alongside embedded job descriptions in Pinecone.
We utilized LangChain to build a dynamic matching and evaluation agent. When a recruiter viewed a job posting, the system performed a semantic similarity search in Pinecone to surface the top 50 candidates, regardless of exact keyword matches. The LangChain agent then used GPT-4 to generate a custom, three-bullet-point summary for each candidate, explicitly explaining why their specific background semantically aligned with the job requirements.
The ROI & Impact: Reduced average time-to-hire for engineering roles from 45 days to 26 days. Increased the interview-to-offer ratio by 22% due to higher quality initial candidate screening.
Total Project Cost: $275,000
