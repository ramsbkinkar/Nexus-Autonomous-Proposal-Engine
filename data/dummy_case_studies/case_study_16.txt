Project Name: Project Omniscience
Industry: Financial Services / Wealth Management
The Client Challenge: A premier wealth management firm struggled to provide personalized, timely advice to high-net-worth clients because client portfolio data, market research, and historical communication logs were trapped in separate, legacy on-premise silos. Advisors spent over two hours manually compiling data from different systems before a single client meeting, severely limiting the number of clients they could effectively manage and increasing the risk of missing critical market signals.
Our Technical Solution: We architected a unified Data Intelligence Platform on AWS, migrating their legacy on-premise SQL databases to Amazon Aurora. We then built a robust data pipeline using Databricks and PySpark to continuously ingest, clean, and unify daily trade logs, CRM notes, and external market feeds into a centralized Delta Lake. This created a single source of truth for all advisor-facing applications.
To augment the advisors' capabilities, we integrated a Generative AI layer using LangChain and OpenAI’s GPT-4. We processed years of the firm’s proprietary market research and financial product brochures, chunking the documents and storing their vector embeddings in Milvus (a high-performance vector database).
We then developed a modern React-based web dashboard. When an advisor searched for a client, the Node.js backend orchestrated a Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) workflow. It queried Milvus for relevant market insights and combined them with the client's real-time portfolio data from Aurora, allowing the LLM to instantly generate a comprehensive, personalized briefing document complete with specific investment recommendations.
The ROI & Impact: Reduced advisor meeting preparation time from over 2 hours to under 10 minutes. Increased assets under management (AUM) by 14% within the first two quarters due to increased advisor capacity.
Total Project Cost: $420,000
